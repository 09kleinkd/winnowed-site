The idea

Most people underestimate what they're paying for subscriptions by a lot. The streaming service you forgot. The tool you used twice. The membership from two years ago.

The usual answer is a spreadsheet, a dashboard of charts, or a cancellation concierge. None of them really work, because the problem isn't a tracking problem — it's a noticing problem. You need a moment, a few times a year, to sit quietly with the list and decide what still belongs.

Winnowed is built around that moment. Add your subscriptions by scanning a statement or typing them in. Then, at a cadence you choose, the app walks you through each one, card by card. Keep. Cut. Pause. That's it.
